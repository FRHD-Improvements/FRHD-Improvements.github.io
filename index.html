Planned Features

I am going to have complete feature parity with the default editor by the first public beta. That being said, these extra features are certainly going to make their way into the editor at some point:

  • More powerful curve tool/editor (See the editor here for an idea).
  • Shapes (circles, squares, hexagons, etc.)
  • Select by points and move/delete
  • Layers

Here are the things I will probably add. I wouldn't bet on them though.

  • Scrub through time (I had a video describing this but it appears that it was deleted üôÅ).
  • Smoother brush option

I am also thinking about possibly having a paid version of the editor once I get it to a good state. The paid version would cost about 5 USD, and it would unlock these features:

  • General track gen
  • Image2Track
  • Shaded brushes (Automatically cross-hatch style shading)

I would also like to add in-editor publishing and riding (essentially creating a third-party client for FRHD) but as that may cause legal issues with Kano (they make lots of ad revenue on their website!) that may not be possible.
